## IMPLEMENTATION INSTRUCTIONS

### How to Create These in Adobe Express:

1. **Go to Adobe Express** (express.adobe.com)
2. **Create New** ‚Üí Instagram Post (1080x1080px)
3. **For each slide:**
   - Add your photography as background
   - Use Raleway font (Bold/ExtraBold for headlines, Regular/Medium for body)
   - Keep text readable with contrast (dark overlay on light images, light overlay on dark images)
   - Maintain brand colors: Teal #2DD4BF, Charcoal #1F2937
4. **Export** as PNG (for Instagram posting)
5. **Upload to Instagram** as carousel (select multiple images when posting)

- **Week 1 Monday:** Carousel 1 - "The Full Spectrum"
