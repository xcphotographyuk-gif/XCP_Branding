# XCP Photography — Brand Stationery & Online Presence Templates
## Complete content for Adobe Express. Every piece pre-written.

**Brand specification for every piece in this file:**
- Background: `#000000` (pure black) or white `#FFFFFF` for internal document pages
- Heading text: `#FFFFFF` on black, `#000000` on white
- Accent / highlight: `#E8E4DC` (warm platinum / white gold)
- Hotel du Vin credential block only: `#d4af37` (gold) — nowhere else
- Font: **Raleway** throughout — weights 300 (body), 400 (regular), 600 (caps/labels), 700 (headings), 800 (hero display)
- Logo: `XCP_Logo_White.png` on black backgrounds, `XCP_Logo_Dark.png` on white backgrounds

**How to use this file:**
1. Open Adobe Express (express.adobe.com) — free account works
2. Create a new project at the dimensions shown for each piece
3. Set the background colour as specified
4. Set font to Raleway (available in Adobe Express)
5. Copy the text content from each section — paste exactly as written
6. Drop your images into the image slots marked **[IMAGE]**
7. Export as PDF (print pieces) or PNG/JPG (digital pieces)

**On desktop:** Save all exported files to your `images/stationery/` folder.
