## Pricing Rationale

### Fine Art Premium Pricing (HDV Collection)
**Justification for £375 - £2,250 range:**

✅ **Labor-Intensive Process:**
- Hand-held long exposure technique requiring expert skill
- Extensive post-processing (image layering and alignment)
- Individual frame curation for optimal sharpness
- Time investment: 8-15 hours per final piece

✅ **Premium Location:**
- Featured in Hotel du Vin Brighton (4-star boutique hotel)
- Central Brighton prime location
- Affluent target demographic
- Exclusive brand association

✅ **Market Positioning:**
- Limited edition status
- Artist signature
- Conservation-grade materials
- Premium framing with museum glass

✅ **Comparable Market:**
- Gallery fine art prints: £300-£3,000+
- Hotel art installations: £500-£5,000
- Limited edition photography: £400-£2,500

**Recommendation:** Pricing is appropriate and well-positioned for the market. Consider:
- Limiting HDV collection to 50 prints per image
- Including edition numbering (e.g., "15/50")
- Offering artist meet-and-greet for A0 purchases
- VIP preview events for new releases

## WooCommerce Integration Notes

**Variable Products Required:**
1. Fine Art Prints - 4 size variants × 3 frame colors × 2 mount options = 24 combinations
2. HDV Exclusives - 4 size variants × 3 premium frame options × 2 mount options = 24 combinations
3. Wall Art - 5 size variants × 3 frame colors × 2 mount options = 30 combinations
4. Mugs - Individual or Set of 4
5. Apparel - Multiple sizes and design options
6. Pop-Up Portraits - 2 session types + add-ons
7. Workshops - 6 workshop types (3 subjects × 2 formats)

**Recommended WooCommerce Setup:**
- Use Product Variations for sizes, frames, mounts
- Create Product Bundles for gift sets
- Set up Booking System integration for sessions/workshops
- Enable Digital Downloads for portrait sessions
- Configure tiered shipping based on print size
