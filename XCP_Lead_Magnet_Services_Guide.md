## PART 1 — COMMERCIAL PHOTOGRAPHY SERVICES

### What XC Photography does

XC Photography specialises in brand and documentary photography for businesses, individuals, and cultural organisations. The work spans corporate commissions, hospitality, music and performance, editorial, and fine art.

Past clients include Norman Cook (Fatboy Slim), the BBC, Amazon, AWS, Hotel du Vin, Malmaison, The Ivy, i360, The Royal Pavilion Brighton, Eddie Izzard, Woody Cook, The Beach Boys, Goldie, Norman Jay OBE, Dean Chalkley, Hugh Cornwell (The Stranglers), Steve Norman (Spandau Ballet), and Gamerspot.

The work is not generic. It is made for clients who want images that reflect something real about who they are — not a corporate brochure version.

### How Pricing Works

XC Photography operates at a minimum session rate of **£1,495**. This is not a day rate — it reflects a full session from preparation through delivery: briefing, technical setup, capture, editing, and licencing.

Larger briefs scale accordingly. Retainer relationships — brands who commission quarterly or annually — attract preferential scheduling and, where appropriate, reduced per-session rates in exchange for forward commitment.

If you are unsure which option fits your budget, the honest answer is: say so in your enquiry. The discovery call is free and there is no pressure to proceed.

### What Happens After You Enquire

1. You fill in the enquiry form at **xcphotography.co/contact** — takes less than 3 minutes.
2. Xavier responds within 24 hours (usually sooner) with any clarifying questions.
3. A free 20-minute discovery call is arranged. No scripts, no pitch.
4. A tailored proposal is sent — specific to your brief, not a generic price list.
5. If it fits, you book. If it doesn't, you haven't lost anything.

## PART 2 — WORKSHOPS & EDUCATION PROGRAMMES

### Why workshops exist

Teaching is part of the practice, not a sideline. The workshops are designed for two different audiences: business owners and marketing professionals who want to create better content themselves, and photographers who want to work in the brand and documentary space professionally.

Every workshop is taught by Xavier Clarke directly. Group sizes are kept deliberately small.

---

### Workshop A — Brand Photography Foundations (Half-Day)

**For:** Business owners, founders, marketing professionals, and content creators who want to shoot better brand content in-house.

**What you learn:**
- Brand photography principles that apply whether you're shooting on a phone or a mirrorless camera
- How to use natural light effectively
- How to direct yourself, your team, or your subject
- What makes an image usable (technically and editorially) — and how to tell the difference
- A simple editing workflow for consistent results
- When DIY is enough, and what to look for when it isn't

**What you leave with:**
- A personal shot list built for your specific brand
- 5+ images shot during the session
- A 30-day follow-up email support window

### Workshop B — Documentary Brand Photography: The Full System (Full Day)

**For:** Photographers wanting to add brand photography to their commercial offer, and advanced content creators who want to work at a professional level.

**Morning — Technical foundation:**
- The documentary photography philosophy applied to commercial work
- Lighting: natural, available, and controlled
- Equipment decisions that matter (and the ones that don't)
- Client communication: briefing, managing expectations, delivering

**Afternoon — Live commission:**
- A real brief, shot in real time
- Each participant works the subject using Xavier's methodology
- Immediate group critique: what works, what doesn't, and why

**What you leave with:**
- Full reference guide to the documentary brand system
- Lightroom preset pack (Xavier's working presets)
- Portfolio review session (one-to-one, within 4 weeks of the workshop)
