## STEP 1: SET UP AMAZON AFFILIATE ACCOUNT (15 Minutes)

**Before you can add affiliate links, you need an account:**

### A. Sign Up for Amazon Associates
1. Go to: **affiliate-program.amazon.co.uk**
2. Click "Join Now for Free"
3. **Enter your account information:**
   - Use your existing Amazon account
   - Or create new one with business email
4. **Add your website:**
   - Website URL: www.xcphotography.co
   - Preferred store ID: `xcphotography-21` (or similar)
5. **Describe your website:**
   - "Photography blog featuring gear reviews, tutorials, and brand photography services"
6. **Choose your niche:**
   - Select: Photography, Electronics, Home & Garden
7. **Explain how you drive traffic:**
   - "SEO, social media, email marketing, networking"
8. **Submit application**

### B. Wait for Approval (Usually 24-48 Hours)
- Amazon will review your application
- You'll receive approval email
- **IMPORTANT:** You must make 3 sales within 180 days to maintain active status

### C. Get Your Associate ID
Once approved:
1. Log in to Amazon Associates dashboard
2. Note your **Associate ID** (e.g., `xcphotography-21`)
3. You'll use this in all your affiliate links

## STEP 2: CREATE AFFILIATE LINKS (20 Minutes per Blog Post)

### How to Generate Amazon Affiliate Links:

**Method 1: SiteStripe (Easiest)**

1. Go to Amazon.co.uk (make sure you're logged into Associates account)
2. Search for product (e.g., "Canon EOS M50")
3. Go to product page
4. **Look for black bar at top** (SiteStripe)
   - If you don't see it, you're not logged into Associates
5. Click "Get Link" in SiteStripe
6. Choose "Text" option
7. **Copy the full link** - looks like:
   ```
   https://www.amazon.co.uk/Canon-EOS-M50/dp/B07XXXXX?tag=xcphotography-21
   ```
8. Save this link - you'll paste it into your blog post

**Method 2: Associates Dashboard**

1. Log into Amazon Associates
2. Tools ‚Üí Product Links
3. Search for product
4. Click "Get Link"
5. Copy the link
6. Save for use in blog post

### Which Products Need Links?

**For EACH blog post, you need links for every product mentioned:**

Example from `BLOG_M43_Music_Photography_Gear.md`:
- Olympus OM-D E-M1 Mark III
- Panasonic Lumix G9
- Olympus M.Zuiko 12-40mm f/2.8 PRO
- Panasonic Leica 25mm f/1.4
- (etc. - every product in the list)

**Create a spreadsheet to organize:**
| Product Name | Amazon Link | Affiliate Link |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| Olympus OM-D E-M1 Mark III | amazon.co.uk/... | (your affiliate link) |

## STEP 3: UPLOAD FIRST BLOG POST (30 Minutes)

### Publishing: BLOG_10_Brand_Photo_Prep_Tips.md

**This post has NO affiliate links - just publish as-is:**

1. **In WordPress:** Posts ‚Üí Add New

2. **Copy content from the .md file:**
   - Open `BLOG_10_Brand_Photo_Prep_Tips.md`
   - Copy ALL text (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C)

3. **Paste into WordPress:**
   - Title: "10 Things to Prepare Before Your Brand Photography Session"
   - Content: Paste the copied text
   - WordPress will auto-format most of it

4. **Format the post:**
   - **Headings:** Make sure ## becomes H2, ### becomes H3
   - **Bullet points:** Should auto-convert
   - **Bold text:** Should auto-convert from **text**
   - If not formatted correctly, select text and use WordPress formatting tools

5. **Add featured image:**
   - Click "Set Featured Image" (right sidebar)
   - Upload a relevant photo from your portfolio
   - Recommended: Behind-the-scenes prep shot OR professional brand photo

6. **Add images throughout post:**
   - Break up text with 2-3 images
   - Use your own photography
   - Add image every 2-3 paragraphs

7. **SEO Settings (Yoast):**
   - Scroll down to Yoast SEO box
   - **Focus Keyword:** "brand photography preparation"
   - **SEO Title:** "10 Things to Prepare Before Your Brand Photography Session"
   - **Meta Description:** "Get amazing results from your brand photography session with this complete preparation checklist. Everything you need to know before shoot day."
   - **Aim for all green lights**

8. **Categories & Tags:**
   - Category: "Photography Tips" or "Brand Photography"
   - Tags: brand photography, preparation, business photography, photoshoot tips

9. **Preview:**
   - Click "Preview" to see how it looks
   - Check mobile view
   - Adjust formatting if needed

10. **Publish:**
    - Click "Publish" button
    - Copy the URL
    - Share on social media!

**‚úÖ YOUR FIRST BLOG POST IS LIVE!**

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### "My affiliate links don't work"
- Check you're logged into Amazon Associates
- Verify your Associate ID is in the link
- Make sure link format is correct: `?tag=yourID-21`

### "Amazon rejected my application"
- Add more content to your website first
- Make sure site has About page, Contact page
- Reapply after adding 3-5 blog posts

### "Blog post formatting looks wrong"
- Switch to "Classic Editor" if using block editor
- Manually format with WordPress tools
- Or paste as plain text and reformat

### "No one is reading my blog"
- Share on social media (multiple times)
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Share in Facebook groups
- Email to your network
- Be patient - SEO takes 3-6 months

### "Not making affiliate sales"
- Add more product links (10+ per post)
- Write better product descriptions
- Show products in action (photos)
- Add comparison tables
- Drive more traffic to posts
