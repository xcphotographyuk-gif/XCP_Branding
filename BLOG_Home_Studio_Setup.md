## Studio Setup Guide: Step-by-Step

### Basic Portrait Lighting (2 Lights)

**Setup:**
1. **Key light:** 45° to left/right of subject, eye level, with softbox
2. **Fill light:** Opposite side, lower power, with umbrella or second softbox

**Settings:**
- **Key light:** 100% power
- **Fill light:** 30-50% power
- **Camera:** ISO 400, f/4, 1/160s

**Result:** Flattering, professional portraits with soft shadows

---

### Three-Point Lighting (3 Lights)

**Setup:**
1. **Key light:** Main light, 45° to side, with softbox
2. **Fill light:** Opposite side, lower power
3. **Rim/Hair light:** Behind subject, aimed at head/shoulders

**Settings:**
- **Key:** 100%
- **Fill:** 30-50%
- **Rim:** 75% (strong enough to separate from background)

**Result:** Professional, dimensional portraits with depth

---

### Dramatic Lighting (2 Lights)

**Setup:**
1. **Key light:** Harsh light with grid or snoot, very directional
2. **Background light:** Low power, colored gel (optional)
3. **No fill light:** Let shadows go dark

**Result:** Editorial, moody, dramatic brand imagery
