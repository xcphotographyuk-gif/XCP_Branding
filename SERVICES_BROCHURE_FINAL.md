### Image Guidelines
⚠️ **DO NOT use Adobe Express AI-generated images**
- Use YOUR professional photography only
- Leave placeholders BLANK until ready
- High-resolution images (300 DPI for print)
- Consistent editing style throughout

### Layout Principles
- Clean, minimalist design
- Generous white space
- Text-image balance 60/40
- Professional, premium feel
- Easy to scan and read

## CREATION WORKFLOW

1. **In Adobe Express:**
   - Create 6 separate 8.5" x 11" (or A4) pages
   - Use Raleway font throughout
   - Leave image areas BLANK initially

2. **Add Your Photography:**
   - Upload your best brand photography work
   - Ensure images are high quality
   - Maintain consistent style/tone

3. **Export Each Page:**
   - PDF format, high quality
   - Individual files: Page1.pdf, Page2.pdf, etc.

4. **Combine PDFs:**
   - Use Adobe Acrobat or free PDF merger
   - Create final: XC_Photography_Services_Brochure.pdf

5. **Use For:**
   - Email to prospects
   - Download on website
   - Print for in-person meetings
   - Share via Instagram DM (save as "SERVICES")
