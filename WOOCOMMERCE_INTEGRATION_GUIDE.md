# WooCommerce Integration & UK Dropshipping Strategy

## Overview
Complete guide to setting up WooCommerce on your WordPress site for selling photography products, merchandise, and affiliate-linked items with UK-based dropshipping suppliers.

---

## PHASE 1: WooCommerce Setup for WordPress

### Step 1: Install WooCommerce Plugin

**WordPress Dashboard → Plugins → Add New**

1. Search "WooCommerce"
2. Click "Install Now" (by Automattic)
3. Click "Activate"
4. Follow setup wizard:
   - Store location: United Kingdom
   - Currency: GBP (£)
   - Sell physical products: Yes
   - Sell digital products: Yes (for presets, templates)

---

### Step 2: Essential WooCommerce Plugins

**Install these for full functionality:**

1. **WooCommerce Stripe Payment Gateway** (FREE)
   - Accept credit/debit cards
   - UK-friendly payment processing

2. **WooCommerce PayPal Checkout** (FREE)
   - Alternative payment method
   - Widely trusted in UK

3. **WooCommerce Amazon Affiliates** (or AAWP Pro - £47-97)
   - Display Amazon products with affiliate links
   - Automatic price updates
   - UK Amazon integration

4. **WooDropship** (£79/year) or **AliDropship** (£89 one-time)
   - Connects to UK dropship suppliers
   - Automated order fulfillment
   - Inventory sync

5. **YITH WooCommerce Wishlist** (FREE)
   - Allows customers to save items
   - Increases conversions

---

### Step 3: Configure Shipping (UK Focus)

**WooCommerce → Settings → Shipping**

**Shipping Zones:**

**Zone 1: UK Mainland**
- Flat rate: £3.95 (standard)
- Free shipping: Orders over £50
- Express: £7.95 (1-2 day)

**Zone 2: Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands, Islands**
- Flat rate: £6.95
- Free shipping: Orders over £75

**Zone 3: Europe**
- Calculated based on weight
- Starting at £12.95

**Zone 4: Rest of World**
- Disable or set very high rate (focus on UK)

---

### Step 4: Tax Configuration (UK VAT)

**WooCommerce → Settings → Tax**

- Enable tax rates: Yes
- Prices entered: Excluding tax
- Display prices: Including tax
- **Standard rate:** 20% VAT (UK)
- Tax class: Standard (for most products)

**Important:** Register for VAT if turnover exceeds £85,000/year

## PHASE 3: Amazon Affiliate Product Integration

### Setup Amazon Associates (UK)

1. **Sign up:** affiliate-program.amazon.co.uk
2. **Create account** with your website URL
3. **Get approved** (can take 24 hours)
4. **Generate affiliate links** for products

---

### WooCommerce Amazon Integration

**Option 1: Manual Product Addition**

**For each Amazon product:**
1. WooCommerce → Products → Add New
2. Product Type: External/Affiliate
3. Add product image (screenshot from Amazon)
4. Description: Your review/recommendation
5. Product URL: Your Amazon affiliate link
6. Button text: "View on Amazon"
7. Price: Amazon price (updates manually)

---

**Option 2: AAWP Plugin (Automated)**

**Plugin:** Amazon Affiliate WordPress Plugin (£47-97/year)

**Features:**
- Automatic price updates
- Product comparisons
- Bestseller lists
- Search functionality
- UK Amazon integration

**Setup:**
1. Install AAWP plugin
2. Enter Amazon API credentials
3. Select UK marketplace
4. Add products via shortcode or blocks

**Example product display:**
```
[amazon box="B0XXXXXXX" template="list"]
```

Creates: Product image, title, price, rating, "Buy on Amazon" button with YOUR affiliate link.

## PHASE 5: Pricing Strategy

### Cost + Margin Calculation

**Print-on-Demand Products:**
- Cost: £10
- Shipping to customer: £3.95 (absorbed if free shipping over £50)
- Transaction fees: 2.9% + £0.30 (Stripe)
- **Total cost:** £10 + £3.95 + fees = ~£14.50
- **Sell price:** £28.99
- **Profit:** £14.49 per sale (50% margin)

---

**Fine Art Prints (Premium):**
- Cost: £25 (A2 framed print)
- Shipping: £6.95
- Fees: 2.9% + £0.30
- **Total cost:** £33
- **Sell price:** £150
- **Profit:** £117 per sale (78% margin)

---

**Affiliate Products:**
- You earn: 3-5% commission
- Example: £500 camera lens
- Commission: £15-25
- Effort: Link creation only
- **Profit margin:** 100% (no cost to you)

---

**Workshops (Highest Margin):**
- Venue cost: £100
- Marketing: £50
- Time investment: 1 day
- **Total cost:** £150
- **Sell price:** £350 per attendee × 10 attendees = £3,500
- **Profit:** £3,350 per workshop (95% margin after costs)

## PHASE 7: Legal & Compliance (UK)

### Required Pages

**Create these WooCommerce pages:**

1. **Terms & Conditions**
   - Returns policy (14-day right to cancel in UK)
   - Shipping times
   - Product guarantees

2. **Privacy Policy**
   - GDPR compliant
   - How customer data is used
   - Cookie policy

3. **Refund Policy**
   - Clear return process
   - Refund timeline
   - Who pays return shipping

4. **Shipping Information**
   - Delivery times by region
   - Tracking information
   - Import duties (if shipping outside UK)

**Use:** WooCommerce Terms Generator (free) or hire solicitor (£300-500)

---

### Business Registration

**If selling products, you need:**

1. **Sole Trader Registration**
   - Register with HMRC
   - Self-assessment tax return
   - FREE to register

2. **VAT Registration** (if turnover >£85k/year)
   - Charge 20% VAT
   - Quarterly returns

3. **Business Bank Account**
   - Separates personal/business finances
   - Easier accounting
