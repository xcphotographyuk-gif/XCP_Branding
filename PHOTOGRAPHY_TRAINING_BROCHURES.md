## Adobe Express Design Specifications

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Raleway ExtraBold, 48-72pt
- Subheads: Raleway Bold, 24-36pt  
- Body: Raleway Regular, 14-18pt
- Pricing: Raleway SemiBold, 20-28pt
- CTAs: Raleway Bold, 16-20pt

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Use your brand colors from website
- Accent: Teal or charcoal for pricing emphasis
- Background: White or light neutral

**Image Placeholders:**
- Workshop brochure: Behind-the-scenes teaching photos, students with cameras, hands-on practice
- Retreat brochure: Brighton coastal scenes, group shooting on location, portfolio review sessions
- 1-2-1 training: Close-up of camera instruction, one-on-one teaching moment, student success shot

**Layout:**
- Keep text blocks readable (max 3-4 lines per section)
- Use plenty of white space
- Pricing should be prominent but not overwhelming
- Include your logo and website on every page

**Export:**
- PDF format for email and website download
- PNG format for social media sharing
- Print-ready PDF for physical brochures
