### 1.1 Homepage Hero Section

**When someone visits xcphotography.co, they should see:**

### 1.2 Above-the-Fold Critical Elements

**Visitor should see WITHOUT scrolling:**
- [ ] What you do (brand photography)
- [ ] Who you serve (ideal client)
- [ ] How to book you (clear CTA button)
- [ ] Professional credibility signal (years in business, number of clients, or testimonial)

**Test:** Stand 6 feet from screen. Can you read/understand all of this?

---

### 1.3 Website Loading Speed

**Test your site:** GTmetrix.com or PageSpeed Insights

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Loads in under 3 seconds on desktop
- [ ] Loads in under 5 seconds on mobile
- [ ] No massive uncompressed images

**If site is slow:** Images are usually the culprit. Compress before uploading.

### 7.2 Contact Form Fields

**Keep it simple (maximum 6 fields):**
- [ ] Name (required)
- [ ] Email (required)
- [ ] Phone (optional)
- [ ] Type of shoot (dropdown: Brand, Headshots, Team, Event, Other)
- [ ] Preferred date (optional)
- [ ] Message (required)

**Remove if present:**
- [ ] Long questionnaires
- [ ] Budget field (discuss later)
- [ ] Detailed project requirements
- [ ] Anything taking >2 minutes to complete

**Every extra field = 10% fewer submissions.**

---

### 7.3 Booking Calendar Integration

- [ ] Calendly link works
- [ ] Opens in new tab
- [ ] Shows your actual availability

- [ ] Confirmation emails sent

**If NOT using Calendly yet:**
- [ ] Sign up (free): calendly.com
- [ ] Create "Free Discovery Call" event
- [ ] Add link to website

**Where to add Calendly:**
- [ ] Homepage CTA button
- [ ] Services page
- [ ] Contact page

- [ ] Business card (QR code)

## COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

### Issue: "No one books after visiting my site"

**Likely problems:**
- No clear CTA
- Hidden/no pricing
- Contact form broken
- Too much friction
- Unclear what you do
- No testimonials/social proof

**Fixes:**
- Add "Book Free Call" button above fold
- Show starting prices
- Test/fix contact form
- Simplify booking process
- Clarify homepage headline
- Add 3+ client testimonials

---

### Issue: "People visit but don't contact me"

**Check:**
- Is contact form working? (test it)
- Is phone number visible?
- Are there multiple ways to contact?
- Is pricing scary/hidden?
- Does portfolio represent what they want?

**Fixes:**
- Make contact ultra-easy
- Show pricing transparency
- Add more CTAs
- Improve portfolio curation

---

### Issue: "Mobile traffic but no bookings"

**Check mobile site:**
- Does everything work?
- Can you tap phone number to call?
- Can you fill out contact form?
- Do images load?
- Is text readable?

**Fix anything broken on mobile first.**
