## HANDLING THE NERVES

### Before You Post:

**Reminder:**
- You're not bragging, you're informing
- People WANT to know you're available
- Someone needs exactly what you offer RIGHT NOW
- Not posting means they'll never know

**Mantra:**
"I'm helping, not selling."

---

### When You're Nervous About Hitting "Post":

**Ask yourself:**
- Would I want to know this if I needed a photographer?
- Am I being helpful or pushy?
- Does this sound like me?

**If yes to all three â†’ Post it.**

---

### After You Post:

**Don't:**
- Refresh constantly checking engagement
- Delete it if it doesn't get immediate likes
- Compare to others' posts
- Overthink it

**Do:**
- Post and forget for 24 hours
- Respond to comments when they come
- Focus on next task
- Remember: Low engagement â‰  failure (you only need 1-2 clients to see it)
