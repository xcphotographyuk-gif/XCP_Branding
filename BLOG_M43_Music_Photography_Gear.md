## Why M43 Is Perfect for Music Photography

You're pressed against a barrier, the bass is rattling your chest, and the artist just stepped into a spotlight that'll last maybe 3 seconds. This is when you realize: size matters. Not bigger—**smaller**.

Micro Four Thirds (M43) gives you everything you need for professional music photography without the shoulder strain, the attention-grabbing bulk, or the "you need a photo pass for that" looks. It's the secret weapon that lets you move fast, shoot clean in terrible light, and capture moments that full-frame shooters are still setting up for.

I've shot over 200 music events with M43 gear. Here's exactly what you need to start capturing professional-quality concert imagery that your clients actually want.

## Why M43 Beats Full-Frame for Music Photography

### 1. **Size & Weight**
- **Carry 3 lenses comfortably** all night
- **Less intimidating** to artists and security

### 2. **Depth of Field Advantage**
- **More in focus at f/2.8** than full-frame at f/2.8
- **Entire band in focus** with f/2.8 wide open
- **Still get isolation** with f/1.2 primes

### 4. **Stabilization**

- **Every lens is stabilized** (in-body)
- **Better for video** (dual IS on compatible lenses)

## Settings for Music Photography

### Camera Settings I Use:
- **Mode:** Manual (M)
- **Shutter:** 1/250s minimum (freezes motion)
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 (balances depth + sharpness)
- **ISO:** Auto, capped at 6,400
- **AF Mode:** C-AF + Tracking
- **Drive Mode:** Sequential High (20fps)
- **File Format:** RAW only

### Why These Settings Work:
**1/250s shutter** freezes most performer movement without motion blur. **f/2.8** keeps enough in focus while gathering light. **Auto ISO** lets me react to changing stage lights instantly.

## Workflow Tips

### During the Show:
1. **First 3 songs** (standard photo pit rule)
2. **Shoot RAW only** (color-correct terrible lighting in post)
3. **Change lenses between songs** (not during)
4. **Check back of camera** every 10-15 shots (exposure check)
5. **Multiple angles** of every moment (you can delete later)

### After the Show:
1. **Back up immediately** (duplicate to second drive)
2. **Cull same night** while memory is fresh
3. **Deliver within 24-48 hours** (promoters love this)
4. **Tag venue/artist on social** (builds relationships)
