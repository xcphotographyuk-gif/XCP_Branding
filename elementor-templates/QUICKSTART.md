# XC Photography Elementor Homepage Template - Quick Start Guide

## Quick Import Steps

1. **WordPress Admin** → **Templates** → **Saved Templates**
2. Click **Import Templates** button
3. Upload: `elementor-templates/xcp-homepage-partial.json`
4. Click **Import Now**
5. Edit your page with Elementor and insert from **My Templates**

## What's Included

✅ **Hero Section** - Full-width cinematic background with H1, H2, subheading, and 2 CTAs  
✅ **Bold Intro Narrative** - Premium boxed text section with Raleway font  
✅ **Masonry Gallery** - 3x3 responsive grid with hover effects  

## First Customizations

### 1. Replace Hero Background Image
- Hero Section Settings → Style → Background
- Upload your 1920x1080px+ hero image

### 2. Update Gallery Images  
- Gallery Widget → Edit images
- Upload 9 portfolio images (mixed dimensions work best)
- Update image titles and alt text

### 3. Update CTA Links
- Button 1: Update link to your contact/booking page
- Button 2: Update link to your portfolio page

### 4. Customize Text
- Edit headings: "Capturing Your Story" and subheading
- Update intro narrative paragraph with your brand story

## Colors Used

- **Gold (Primary):** `#d4af37` - CTA buttons, accents
- **Dark:** `#1a1a1a` - Hero background overlay
- **Light Gray:** `#f8f8f8` - Section backgrounds  
- **White:** `#ffffff` - Content boxes, text on dark
- **Dark Gray:** `#333333` - Body text

## Font

**Raleway** - Used throughout (weights: 400, 600, 700)

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Desktop:** 3 columns, 72px headings
- **Tablet:** 2 columns, 48px headings  
- **Mobile:** 1 column, 36px headings
