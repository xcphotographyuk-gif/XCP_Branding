## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (1140px+)
- Hero: 72px H1, 36px H2
- Intro: 20px body text
- Gallery: 3 columns

### Tablet (768px - 1139px)
- Hero: 48px H1, 28px H2
- Intro: 18px body text
- Gallery: 2 columns

### Mobile (< 768px)
- Hero: 36px H1, 22px H2
- Intro: 16px body text
- Gallery: 1 column (stacked)
