**Why every brand photographer needs one:**

- **Portable:** Collapses to 12 inches, weighs nothing
- **Instant light control:** Add fill, block light, warm tones, cool tones
- **No power needed:** Works anywhere, anytime

**The surfaces and what they do:**

**1. White (Soft Fill)**
- Bounces subtle, neutral fill light
- Perfect for reducing shadows on faces
- Natural-looking results
- **Use when:** Outdoor portraits, window light portraits

**2. Silver (Maximum Reflection)**
- Bright, contrasty fill
- Adds sparkle to eyes (catchlight)
- Cool tone
- **Use when:** Need maximum light fill, overcast days

**3. Gold (Warm Fill)**
- Adds warm, sunset-like glow
- Flattering skin tones
- Creates golden hour feel anytime
- **Use when:** Want warm, inviting imagery

- Blocks light and reflections
- Adds depth and shadows
- Creates dramatic, moody images
- **Use when:** Controlling window light, adding dimension

**5. Diffusion (Soft Light)**
- Turns harsh sunlight into soft, flattering light
- Reduces contrast
- Eliminates harsh shadows
- **Use when:** Midday sun, backlit scenarios
