# Images for XCP Photography — social

Background images for social media content — Instagram stories, LinkedIn banner, Adobe Express templates.

## Files to add:

| Filename | Use | Dimensions |
|---|---|---|
| `linkedin-banner-bg.jpg` | LinkedIn profile banner (right half) | 800 × 396px |
| `instagram-story-bg-01.jpg` | Instagram story background | 1080 × 1920px |
| `instagram-story-bg-02.jpg` | Instagram story background — alternate | 1080 × 1920px |
| `instagram-highlight-bg.jpg` | Highlight cover background (optional — can use plain black) | 1080 × 1920px |

## Rules:
- LinkedIn banner: dark portrait or editorial — the right half of the 1584×396px banner
- Instagram stories: dark backgrounds work best with white text overlays
- PNG or JPG

## How to add on desktop:
Drop images here → open Adobe Express → upload from this folder when building each template.
Full template specs in: `XCP_BRAND_STATIONERY_TEMPLATES.md`
