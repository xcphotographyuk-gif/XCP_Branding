# Images for XCP Photography — stationery-use

Background images used inside stationery pieces — business card front, proposal cover, welcome card. These are NOT portfolio images — they are the dark, moody backgrounds that make the stationery look premium.

## Files to add:

| Filename | Used in | Dimensions | What to use |
|---|---|---|---|
| `business-card-bg.jpg` | Business card — front | 1004 × 650px | Dark, minimal — detail shot, texture, or abstract |
| `proposal-cover-bg.jpg` | Proposal / quote cover page | 2480 × 3508px | Strong editorial portrait, landscape fill |
| `welcome-card-bg.jpg` | Client welcome card — front | 1418 × 1004px | Warm but editorial — a great shoot moment |

## Rules:
- These images will have dark overlays applied in Adobe Express — they don't need to be perfectly dark on their own
- Avoid busy compositions — they'll compete with the text
- JPG

## How to add on desktop:
Drop images here → open Adobe Express → upload from this folder when building each stationery piece.
Full specs in: `XCP_BRAND_STATIONERY_TEMPLATES.md`
