### Customization

All templates are fully customizable through Elementor's visual editor:
- Replace placeholder images with your photography
- Update text content to match your brand
- Adjust colors and typography to your preference
- Modify layouts and spacing as needed

## Brand Guidelines

- **Primary Gold:** #d4af37

- **White:** #ffffff
- **Text Dark:** #333333

- Clean, modern aesthetic suitable for professional photography
