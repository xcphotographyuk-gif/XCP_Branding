# Warm Outreach Templates - XCP Brand Voice
## For People Who Hate "Selling" (Non-Sales-y Approach)

**Your situation:** You have booking form + CTAs on site. You just need to reach out to people authentically.

**Your challenge:** Low confidence, don't want to sound sales-y, need to match your brand voice.

**Solution:** These templates feel like genuine connection, not selling.

---

## MINDSET SHIFT (Read This First)

**You're NOT selling. You're:**
- Letting people know you have availability
- Offering to help solve a problem they might have
- Starting conversations with people you care about
- Sharing something valuable

**XCP Brand Voice:**
- Authentic over polished
- Helpful over pushy
- Personal over corporate
- Story-driven over feature-driven
- "I noticed..." over "I'm offering..."

---

## OPTION 1: NEWSLETTER APPROACH (Lowest Pressure)

### Why This Works for You:
- One message reaches many people
- Feels less "sales-y" than individual emails
- Educational/valuable content first, offer second
- People opt-in by reading, no pressure to respond

## SCRIPTS FOR LOW CONFIDENCE MOMENTS

### When You Feel Awkward Reaching Out:

**Remind yourself:**
- "I'm not selling. I'm letting someone know I can help."
- "The worst they can say is 'not right now.'"
- "People who need this will be grateful I reached out."
- "I'd want someone to tell me if they could help with something I need."

---

### Opening Line That Feels Natural:

```
"Not sure if this is on your radar, but I have some photography 
availability in [Month] and thought of you."
```

**Why it works:**
- Acknowledges it might not be relevant ("not sure if...")
- Personal ("thought of you")
- Information, not sales pitch
- Easy to decline

---

### If They Say "Not Right Now":

```
"No worries at all! If anything changes or you know someone who needs 
photos, feel free to send them my way. Either way, hope you're doing well!"
```

**Then move on. No awkwardness.**

## NEWSLETTER VS. INDIVIDUAL EMAILS

### Start With Newsletter If:
- You have 50+ contacts
- You're uncomfortable with individual outreach
- You want to share value regularly
- You don't need immediate bookings

**Tool:** MailerLite (free up to 1,000 subscribers)

---

### Do Individual Emails If:
- You have 10-30 warm contacts
- You need bookings THIS MONTH
- You can customize messages
- You're willing to be slightly uncomfortable

**Hybrid approach:** Newsletter monthly + individual emails to hottest leads
