## Visual Identity Essentials

### Logo Usage

**Primary Logo: XCP Photography**
- Use on: Website, business cards, contracts, official documents
- Minimum size: 120px width (digital) | 1.5" width (print)
- Clear space: 20% of logo height on all sides

**Logo Colors:**
- Primary: Charcoal Black (#1A1A1A)
- Reverse: Pure White (#FFFFFF)
- Always use high-contrast placement

**What NOT to do:**
- ‚ùå Don't stretch or distort
- ‚ùå Don't add effects or shadows
- ‚ùå Don't change colors
- ‚ùå Don't place on busy backgrounds

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Charcoal Black** - #1A1A1A (main color, 50% of usage)
- **Pure White** - #FFFFFF (backgrounds, negative space, 20% of usage)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Deep Slate** - #2C3E50 (secondary elements, 20% of usage)
- **Warm Grey** - #95A5A6 (body text, subtle elements, 10% of usage)
- **Accent Gold** - #D4AF37 (highlights only, maximum 5% of usage)

### Typography

**Headlines:** Montserrat Bold (700)
- Use for: Hero headlines, page titles, major headers
- Sizes: 48-72px (large), 36-48px (medium)

**Body Text:** Open Sans Regular (400)
- Use for: Paragraphs, descriptions, content
- Size: 16-18px for readability

## Photography Style

### Our Signature Look
**"Cinematic Documentary with Bold Authenticity"**

**Key Characteristics:**
1. **High Contrast** - Rich blacks, luminous highlights
2. **Emotional Depth** - Genuine moments over posed shots
3. **Bold Composition** - Dynamic angles, intentional framing
4. **Authentic Feel** - Real stories, real people
5. **Cinematic Quality** - Film-inspired aesthetic

### Lighting Style
- Natural light preferred (golden hour, window light)
- Dramatic contrast with depth and dimension
- Moody atmosphere when appropriate
- Never flat or overly bright

### What We Capture
- **Authentic expressions** over forced smiles
- **Natural poses** with gentle direction
- **Environmental context** - subjects in their element
- **Emotional moments** that tell your story
- **Detail shots** that reveal quality and craftsmanship

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I modify the logo?
No. Please use only the provided logo files without modifications. If you need a specific variation, contact the XCP Photography team.

### What if I need a color not in the palette?
The brand palette is carefully chosen for consistency. If you have a specific need, please consult with the team before introducing new colors.

### Can I use my own fonts?
For official XCP Photography branded materials, please use only Montserrat and Open Sans. For your own client materials featuring our imagery, you may use your brand fonts.

### How do I get high-resolution images?
High-resolution final images are delivered to clients upon project completion. Portfolio samples are available upon request for approved partners.

### Can I share my project photos on social media?
Absolutely! We encourage clients to share their final imagery. Please provide photo credit to XCP Photography when possible.

### What if something doesn't look right?
If any branded material doesn't align with these guidelines, please reach out. We're happy to provide guidance or make corrections.
