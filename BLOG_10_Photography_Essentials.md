## Essential #3: Backup Storage Solution

**Your photos are worthless if they're lost.**

Every professional photographer has the same nightmare: Hard drive failure. Years of work—gone.

### The Rule: 3-2-1 Backup
- **3 copies** of every file
- **2 different storage types** (SSD + HDD, or cloud + local)
- **1 off-site location** (cloud or physical backup at different location)

### Minimum Investment: £100
**Seagate 4TB External HDD** (£90) + **Backblaze Cloud Backup** (£6/month)
