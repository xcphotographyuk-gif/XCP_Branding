## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS FOR ALL CAROUSELS

### Typography (Adobe Express - Raleway Font)
- **Headlines:** Raleway Bold or Raleway ExtraBold
- **Body text:** Raleway Regular or Raleway Medium
- **Accent/CTA text:** Raleway SemiBold

### Brand Colors
- **Primary:** Teal #2DD4BF
- **Secondary:** Charcoal #1F2937
- **Background:** White #F9FAFB
- **Accent:** Gold #F59E0B (for CTAs)

### Image Guidelines
‚ö†Ô∏è **DO NOT use Adobe Express AI-generated images**
- Use YOUR authentic photography
- Leave placeholders BLANK until you upload your images
- Mix: brand photography, documentary moments, behind-the-scenes, results

### Layout Principles
- Clean, minimalist design
- Generous white space
- Text readable on mobile (test at 1080x1080px)
- Images lead, text supports
- Consistent branding throughout

### Export Settings
- **Format:** PNG for Instagram feed posts
- **Size:** 1080 x 1080px (square)
- **Quality:** High/Maximum
