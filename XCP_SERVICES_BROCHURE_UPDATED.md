- **Headlines:** Raleway Bold / ExtraBold
- **Subheadings:** Raleway SemiBold  
- **Body text:** Raleway Regular / Medium
- **Accent text:** Raleway Medium

### Colors
- **Primary:** Teal #2DD4BF
- **Secondary:** Charcoal #1F2937
- **Background:** White #FFFFFF
- **Accent:** Use your photography as color inspiration

### Image Specifications
- **Cover:** Full bleed, impactful brand photography
- **Interior pages:** Mix of full-bleed images and contained images with white space
- **Back page:** Strong portrait or success story image

### Layout Principles
- Clean, minimalist design
- Generous white space
- Professional, high-end aesthetic  
- Images lead, text supports
- Client-benefit focused messaging throughout

- **Binding:** Saddle-stitched OR flat if printing individual sheets
